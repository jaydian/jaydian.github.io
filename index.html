About us!

Nothern Mill represents diversity,equity & unity!
Nothern Mill was founded in the year 2000 by Mila Gill. A middle aged black lady wanting to create a safe space for all of different race, religion and gender.
As a child she was bulied for being a black girl and her features that came with it. Through this experience her motive qas to create chamge within society which starts with building a safe space for the youth.
We welcome all to express your true indentity as well as feeling free to explore the possibilites of your future!
To support this, we offer many programs regarding different carrer paths for all

  • AP
  • SHSM
  • Biotechnology Program
  • Extended French Program
  • STEAM
  • Dance
  • NM Soccer
  • Flying Eagles Track Club

With the wide variety of programs, we allow our students to open their minds and prevent them for feeling narrowly directed to wellknown carrer paths. With the variety students may feel overwhelmed so we highly encourge students to take a visit to our guidance office to discuss their fit!

About Location:

Nothern Mills is located within a area of large community & support.
As we are located within the Toronto region(7777 Gates ave.) we are close neighbors to Rosedale River Secondary school, Nothern community center as well as St.Ans Catholic church.
With this suppport we are able to partake in many activities as we allow our students to collaborate and develop and relatioship with society as they will do in the future. We are also able to gain fundraisable support and also give back as we are apart of this united community!
