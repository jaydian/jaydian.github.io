Welcome to Nothern Mill Secondary School!

About us!:

Nothern Mill strives to represent diversity,equity & unity!
Northern Mill was founded in the year 2000 by Mila Gill. A middle-aged black woman wanting to create a safe space for all of different race, religion, and gender.
As a child, Mrs.Gill was bullied for being a black girl and the features that came with it. Through this experience, her motive was to create change within society which starts with building a safe space for the youth.
We welcome all to express your true identity as well as feel free to explore the possibilities of your future!
To support this, we offer many programs regarding different career paths for all

  • AP
  • SHSM
  • Biotechnology Program
  • Extended French Program
  • Psychology Department
  • STEAM
  • Dance
  • NM Soccer
  • Flying Eagles Track Club

With the wide variety of programs, we allow our students to open their minds and prevent them from feeling narrowly directed to well-known career paths. With the variety students may feel overwhelmed so we highly encourage students to take a visit to our guidance office to discuss their fit!

About Location:

Nothern Mills is located within an area of large community & support. As we are located within the Toronto region(7777 Gates Ave.) we are close neighbors to Rosedale River Secondary School, Nothern community center as well as St.Ans Catholic church.
With this support we are able to partake in many activities as we allow our students to collaborate and develop and relationship with society as they will do in the future. We are also able to gain fundraise support and also give back as we are a part of this united community!
