~Student life at Nothern Mill~

Many schools only talk about the programs provided but we want you to enter our school feeling comfortable with the environment and routine we go by!

Let's take a further look!

From 7:40-8:20 breakfast is provided for all students, first come first serve!
We acknowledge the importance of students balancing healthy life with education and with that, first comes necessary nutrition!
We are able to provide this as we are funded by the minister of education, Stephen Lecce.

Classes:

Class begins at 8:30 with a duration of an hour, ten mins(4 periods)!
  • 8:30am-9:40am
  • 9:45am-10:55am
  • 11:00am-12:10pm
  • Lunch:12:12pm-1:10pm
  • 1:15pm-2:25pm

With this, Every Tuesday and Friday we hold a study session concerning help for students struggling within classes.
Each day addresses different subjects which are posted outside the library every Monday and Wednesday!
We acknowledge that students are in need of guidance at times and it's our responsibility to do so.
