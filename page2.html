~Flying Eagles Track Club~

Although Northern Mills has multiple programs to allow all to explore different career paths , we did not forget about the athletics!
We especially specialize in track and field which introduces our club name "Flying Eagels"!

Why join Flying Eagles?

At Flying Eagles, we focus and take crucial training to each individual event

  • Short Sprinters
  • Middle Distance
  • Long Distance
  • Shot Putt
  • Hurderling
  • High Jump
  • Long Jump/Tripple Jump
  • Pole Vault
  • Discus
  • Javelin
  • Hammer Throw
  • 4x1,4x2 & 4x4
